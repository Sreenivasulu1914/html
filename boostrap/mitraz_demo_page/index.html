About Us

MITRAz (Moulding Intellect through TRaining And Zeal ) is a Sec.12AA-registered public charitable trust established in 2017 in Puttaparthi, Anantapur Dist. Andhra Pradesh. MITRAz draws its inspiration from Bhagwan Sri Sathya Sai Baba and His magnanimous projects for the welfare of mankind.

As passionate educationists, we provide free skill development courses to students from rural backgrounds under our flagship program MITRAz Skills with the view to uplifting young lives. This first-of-its-kind project focusses on imparting a holistic blend of technical skills, soft skills and human values

As an NGO working at the grassroots level, we have identified and initiated several service activities to deal with acute community problems. Amongst these is Mitravaani, our 24/7 free cellphone radio service that provides credible information to digitally challenged communities.

Driven by a determined spirit of seva, we are constantly on the lookout for opportunities to engage meaningfully with our community and make the world a better place.
