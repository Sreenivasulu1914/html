About Us

At MITRAz (Moulding Intellect through TRaining And Zeal ), is a Sec.12AA-registered public charitable trust formed in 2017 in Puttaparthi, Anantapur Dist. Andhra Pradesh. The founders and the Director have a rich experience in volunteering and value-based initiatives of Education, under the Sathya Sai group of institutions. As passionate educationists, we provide skill development courses (free of cost) to students from rural backgrounds. MITRAz Skill aims to uplift young lives by providing standardized skill training for those who have basic education, to become self-sufficient. It is a project that is first of its kind in Puttaparthi which focuses on imparting a blend of technical skills, soft skills and human values. In essence, MITRAz is not only committed to creating confident/ transformed rural youth but also rural communities. As an NGO working at the grassroots level in rural India, we continuously identify and initiate numerous service activities to deal with pressing community problems that require immediate attention. In our pursuit towards social work to serve our community, we launched First Steps- Connect, Grow, Bond Program on 19th January for year 9-12 Government School students with a focus on skilling them. The vision of MITRAz Foundation is to bring about a sustainable change in the lives of young men and women through an innovative curriculum that helps develop careers. We wish to focus on career guidance (through counsellors and personality development modules) so that the participants can chart out their success journey. Link to First Steps – Connect, Grow, Bond page Starting April 2023, we have extended MITRAz Skills training in IT and Analytics sector co-branding with interested Analytics / Consulting firms in Corporate India. At MITRAz, we see great opportunities in this domain and are keen on creating a program that would help usher change for businesses. Driven by a determined spirit of seva, we are constantly on the lookout for opportunities to engage meaningfully with our community and make the world a better place. Our Vision

In addition to the vision and mission, our work is driven by a set of core values. These core values are our foundation and it defines who we are and what we do. We encourage MITRAites to internalise and practice these core values. Commitment At MITRAz, we are committed to providing a conducive environment that stimulates learning, empowering the rural youth and the community. Excellence At MITRAz, excellence is a way of life. We strive to provide best-in-class learning for our participants and finest quality of life for our communities. Integrity At MITRAz, we serve a variety of stakeholders and are accountable to all. We demonstrate integrity in every word, action and deed. We also demonstrate through the unwavering commitment to the community through a host of community initiatives. Caring At MITRAz, we demonstrate caring by developing a shared purpose, mission and vision with our stakeholders. We also aim to provide outstanding developmental opportunities for our people.
