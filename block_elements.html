At MITRAz (Moulding Intellect through TRaining And Zeal ), is a Sec.12AA-registered public charitable trust formed in 2017 in Puttaparthi, Anantapur Dist. Andhra Pradesh. The founders and the Director have a rich experience in volunteering and value-based initiatives of Education, under the Sathya Sai group of institutions. As passionate educationists, we provide skill development courses (free of cost) to students from rural backgrounds. MITRAz Skill aims to uplift young lives by providing standardized skill training for those who have basic education, to become self-sufficient. It is a project that is first of its kind in Puttaparthi which focuses on imparting a blend of technical skills, soft skills and human values. In essence, MITRAz is not only committed to creating confident/ transformed rural youth but also rural communities.
